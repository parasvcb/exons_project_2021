Tutorial NEXTRaP database

Tutorial:

From Home page, gene can be queried using either the Gene ID (Entrez NCBI) or using its name

After selecting the Gene from the listed drop down list, following information will be rendered

  • The result page will be headed by the Gene name and organism. Information about the transcripts is given in the 'TRANSCRIPT TABLE'. Exons are also segregated into five different categories of UTMRD and three of AGF in that table for every transcript.

  • 'EXON TABLE' contains the detailed description of every exon in all the transcripts along with their descriptive nomenclature (Exon Id), Raw and Coding genomic coordinates (RGSC,RGEC - Raw Genomic start/end coordinates; CGSC,CGEC- Coding genomic start/end coordinates), length in amino acids and weighted exon frequency (WEF).

  • The color coded Pfam annotated domains can be seen in the 'DOMAIN TABLE'. After this table, all the transcripts are listed and their details can be seen by clicking on 'SHOW' button.

  • After clicking on 'SHOW' button against the transcript, exon details can be seen. For non-coding exons, content is displayed by #, whereas for coding exons it is single letter aa code.

  • The secondary structure of the exons can be highlighted on the exons by clicking on 'Show SS', yellow is 'Beta sheet region' and purple is 'Alpha helix region', and '-' are unordered secondary structure type.

  • The Pfam domains of the exons can be visualised by clicking on 'Show Dom', region not colored or in '-' is not coding any domains.
